What Is Dropshipping?

Dropshipping is an order fulfilment method for retail businesses whereby they do not need to purchase or hold stock. Instead, at Jewellery Dropship we allow you to customise our wide range of products, and we'll ship the order directly to your customer. Focus on your branding and selling products – we’ll handle the rest!
